Project Description

In the midst of the greens of Ali-bag is cited the project ‘alibag ONE‘. Vidyanagar is an upscale locality of Alibag originally planned for the respected teaching commuity of Alibag and is still as soothing as it was planned. Surrounded by small cottages and bungalows, this locality is one of the most peaceful locations of Alibag and at the same time extremely close to the city-centre and a walking distance to the natures of Varsoli Beach and its authentic ‘Suru forests’.
A very talented team ensures efficient planning, great design and quality output. A Charles Correa Award-winning Architect, Structural Consultant from USA, IT Experts and top management with interna-tional diverse experience are part of a team work-ing on ‘alibag ONE‘. Home Automation

Imagine you are in a room and all the electrical device comforts are at yourfinger tips.Where you need not budge from your comfortable station and control the lights, fans, curtains, blinds and all the gadgets in the room. Be it the switching them on,the intensity-control of all the myriad functions, or the switching-off of every electrical unit in one go. Now stop imagining. Because all of this is now a reality called Digital Dreams. So what exactly are these Digital Dreams?
In simple terms, they are touch-sensitive Control Pads. Or, in other words, Intelligent Switches. That means the most basic models are the modern substitute for conventional switches and switchboards. Moreover, they are always at an easy reach even in the dark, thanks to a soft lit-up display. In addition, they come in sleek and stylish designs that can take the luxury quotient of your home a notch higher.
